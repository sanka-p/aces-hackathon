What is ACES Hackathon?

ACES Hackathon, organised by the Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) of the Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya, is an ambitious and awe-inspiring event for the first time in ACES history. The main objective of this event is to show the seeds of entrepreneurship in young undergraduates in view of inspiring them to become world leaders in the IT industry.
ACES Hackathon is a software development competition with the purpose to inspire young undergraduates to develop innovative and creative AI solutions in a competitive environment. ACES Hackathon will be an excellent platform for young & talented undergraduates of the University of Peradeniya to expose their innovative and novel ideas to the industry and the world.
